AmiBroker Formula Language (AFL)

AmiBroker is equipped with a powerful formula language allowing you to write trading system rules, define your own indicators and custom commentaries. This chapter explains the language, gives you a detailed reference of built-in analysis functions and shows how to use AFL tools such as the automatic analyzer and formula editor.
